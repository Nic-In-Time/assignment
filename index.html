Search Engines and LLMs

Search Engines

Search Engines take in keywords, and give you webpages that have those keywords. The pages are ordered using several factors, including how often it is visited, among other factors

LLMs

The LLMs start by tokenizing words. This means that the words are turned into numbers that represent them. The numbers are a vector, representing the location of the word. This is used to predict the next word, that next word being the one closest to them without having transformers. These vectors also take into account the words before the one that is getting tokenized, making LLMs different from a basic prediction in, say, an SMS app. The vectors are not just 2d, or even 3d, but can stretch hundreds of dimentions. Transformers are used to add into the equation previous wordsin the sentence.
